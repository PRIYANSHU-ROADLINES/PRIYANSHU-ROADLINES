About Us

PRIYANSHU ROADLINES was established in 2023 with a vision to provide reliable, safe and efficient transportation services across India.


The Proprietor of Priyanshu Roadlines, Mr. Chandan Chaudhary, brings more than 21 years of experience in the transportation and logistics industry. His expertise and dedication have helped the company build strong relationships with customers and business partners.


We provide fast and safe transportation services all over India. Our commitment to timely deliveries, customer satisfaction and secure handling of goods makes us a trusted logistics partner for businesses.


Currently, we are proudly providing transportation services to several reputed companies including Luxor Writing Pvt. Ltd., Forace Polymer Pvt. Ltd. and many others.


At Priyanshu Roadlines, we are committed to making sure your packages and consignments arrive quickly, safely and efficiently.

Our Mission

To provide safe, timely and cost-effective transportation services while maintaining the highest standards of customer satisfaction and reliability.

Our Vision

To become one of India's most trusted logistics and transportation partners by delivering excellence in every shipment.
